About me

Designer who actually ships.

Hey, I'm Paige Simm. I'm a 24-year-old interface designer from New Zealand with a Bachelor of Design (Interface Design) from the University of Waikato. I have a habit of making things look significantly better than I found them.

Most recently I was a Marketing Coordinator at a luxury lighting company where I rebuilt their website from scratch, created print collateral, ran social media, and made sure everything from spec sheets to in-building signage felt polished. Before that I did a design internship, and spent three years at NZ Post sneaking in design work wherever I could.

I sit at the intersection of visual design and front-end development and I'm equally comfortable in Figma or a code editor. Oh, and every graphic, illustration, and image across my work is hand-drawn by me in Illustrator.
